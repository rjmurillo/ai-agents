# Principal Engineer Knowledge Index (15+ Years)

**Created**: 2026-01-10
**Category**: Engineering Knowledge Management
**Comprehensive Index**: [engineering-knowledge-index](engineering-knowledge-index.md)
**Consolidated from**: Sessions 816 and 819

## Purpose

Knowledge framework for Senior Staff+, Principal Engineers, and Architects operating at systems leadership level. Focus shifts from individual contribution to organizational effectiveness and long-term leverage.

## Core Role Shift

At 15+ years, optimization targets change:
- Individual output → System health over time
- Personal contribution → Org effectiveness
- Technical correctness → Clarity and alignment in ambiguity
- Feature delivery → Good decisions under incomplete information
- My project → Technical vision aligned with strategy

## Wisdom Over Practices

| Concept | Key Insight | Quick Reference |
|---------|-------------|-----------------|
| Chesterton's Fence | Understand before removing | Investigate existing rationale |
| Conway's Law | Architecture mirrors org structure | Inverse Conway Maneuver |
| Paved Roads & Guardrails | Enable, don't constrain | Balance standards with flexibility |
| Scaling Laws | Coordination costs grow nonlinearly | Use bounded contexts |
| Ownership vs Leverage | Enable others, don't do it yourself | Focus on multiplier effects |
