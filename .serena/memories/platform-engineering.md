# Platform Engineering

**Created**: 2026-01-10
**Category**: Developer Experience

## Overview

Discipline of designing and building toolchains and workflows enabling self-service capabilities for engineering organizations.

## Core Concepts

| Concept | Definition |
|---------|------------|
| **IDP** | Internal Developer Platform: tools for developer self-service |
| **Golden Paths** | Standardized, secure, scalable self-service capabilities |
| **Platform-as-Product** | Treat internal platforms like external products |

## Benefits

- Reduces cognitive load on developers
- Provides abstraction over infrastructure complexity
- Enables focus on building and delivering software
- Standardizes security and scalability

## Self-Service Model

Digital store analogy: Developers are customers. Platform provides self-service experiences. Platform engineers set up catalog of items with organizational guardrails.

## Industry Adoption

Gartner expects 80% of engineering organizations to have dedicated platform engineering teams by 2026.

## Platform Team Responsibilities

- Build internal developer portals
- Implement self-service workflows
- Improve performance and observability
- Work with engineers and business stakeholders
- Evolve platform to meet changing requirements
