# Knowledge Integration Quality Rubric

## Scoring (1-5 scale)

| Score | Label | Definition |
|-------|-------|------------|
| 1 | Generic | No domain-specific content. Could be answered without any skill knowledge. |
| 2 | Shallow | Mentions the right concepts but lacks depth, examples, or actionable guidance. |
| 3 | Adequate | Covers the topic with reasonable accuracy. Missing nuance or advanced cases. |
| 4 | Strong | Accurate, specific, includes examples or decision criteria. Minor gaps. |
| 5 | Expert | Precise, actionable, includes edge cases, tradeoffs, and when-not-to-use. Matches wiki depth. |

## Dimensions

Each prompt scored on 3 dimensions:
- **Accuracy**: Does the answer contain correct domain knowledge?
- **Depth**: Does it go beyond surface-level into decision criteria, tradeoffs, edge cases?
- **Specificity**: Does it give concrete examples, not just abstract principles?

Final score = average of 3 dimensions, rounded to nearest 0.5.
