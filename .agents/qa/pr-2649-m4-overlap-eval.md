The `build_report_md` function previously emitted a trailing blank line before the final newline,
causing markdownlint MD012/MD047 to fail on every generated `REPORT.md`. The fix strips the
trailing blank lines before writing. The generated artifact on this branch passes lint without
any manual post-edit.

**Canonical-source principle**: generator fixed; artifact not hand-edited. Future runs will
produce clean output without intervention.
